About Us

'Retro Gamers' is a traditional arcade situated in Birmingham that specialises in older upright arcade machines featuring classics such as 'Pac-Man', 'Space-Invaders', 'Street Fighter' and many more. Most of the machines have been salvaged and repaired by our crew giving us a different assortment each month!

Unlike the traditional arcades of old, no quarters are needed! Alternate controllers can also be provided to accomodate everyone. No food or drink is provided on premises however feel free to bring your own. Entry costs vary per night (Please see below) for non-members, or alternatively sign up for monthly membership.

For any other questions feel free to contact us using the details at the bottom of the page.
